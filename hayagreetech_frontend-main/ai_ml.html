What Are AI and ML?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence that is occurring in the machines so they act like humans. Machines are enabled by AI to perform such tasks as making decisions, solving problems and understanding human language.

Machine Learning (ML) is a part of AI that is based upon statistical methods and aims at facilitating computer training based on data without terming that as explicit programming effort. ML algorithms analyze patterns in data, make predictions and give suggestions with a view to optimizing business results.
