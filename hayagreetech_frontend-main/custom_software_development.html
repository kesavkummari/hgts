Our Custom Software Development Process

Consultation & Requirement Analysis : We start the entire process by gathering information on your business as well as goals and specific requirements through detailed inquiry and analysis as this aids in establishing limits of the project and drafting and detailed roadmap.

Design & Prototyping : Our designers and developers team up to build a prototype that captures your ideas. The software is application orientated where the looks, feel and operation of the software is perfect.

Development : As for our development team, they follow the latest trends, technologies and standards so as to create strong, flexible and working systems. We update you frequently at all stages and receive relevant comments to ensure that the solution developed is in line with your objectives.

Testing & Quality Assurance : This is also the stage where the utmost testings are undertaken so as to ascertain that the software does not have any defects, security flaw, or does not work beyond or under any working conditions. This includes functional testing, performance testing, security testing and user acceptance testing (UAT).

Deployment & Integration : We are responsible for the deployment of the software ensuring no disruption of your business activities as well as that of the organization by integrating the new software with already in existence ones and the environment of operation.

Maintenance & Support : It is at the point of software deployment that the two companies begin active partnership. We also provide further maintenance and support services in order for your software to remain up to date secure and efficient.
